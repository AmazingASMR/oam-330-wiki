## Definition
Big 5 traits predict job performance, but the relationships are **reliable yet small, and mostly contingent (job-dependent) rather than direct**. Conscientiousness is the lone consistent across-the-board predictor.

## Why it matters
This is the high-yield application of OCEAN. Williams's framing — "personality–performance relationships are reliable but small; strongest relationships are contingent rather than direct" — is the exam-line.

## Key distinctions
- **C is direct, the rest are contingent** — Conscientiousness predicts performance everywhere (persistence, organization, fewer errors); the rest depend on job demands.
- **vs. General mental ability** — Across most jobs GMA is a stronger predictor than any personality trait (Schmidt & Hunter — see Myers reading).
- **vs. MBTI claims** — MBTI markets type-to-job matches but doesn't actually predict performance; OCEAN does, modestly ([[concepts/myers-briggs]]).

## Per-trait summary
- **O — Openness** — predicts creativity, flexibility, adaptation to change; may make better leaders (handle ambiguity); *overall job-performance relationship is minimal*.
- **C — Conscientiousness** — **strongest direct predictor**; persistence, organization, planning, fewer errors; not contingent on job type or rank.
- **E — Extraversion** — predicts performance **only** in jobs requiring interpersonal contact or teamwork (sales, management); may make better leaders.
- **A — Agreeableness** — predicts more liking by co-workers, less deviant behavior; predicts performance **only** in interpersonal/teamwork jobs.
- **N — Neuroticism** — predicts less job and life satisfaction, more stress, negativity, more difficulty managing workplace stress.

## Example(s)
- Salespeople and managers: high E + high A pay off (interpersonal contact).
- Truck driver, accountant: high C matters; E and A do not.

## Likely MCQ angles
"Which Big 5 trait is the most consistent predictor of job performance across roles?" → **Conscientiousness**. Distractor: Extraversion (only contingent). Distractor: Openness (minimal). Scenario: a sales manager hire — flag E + A as relevant.
